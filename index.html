About Us

Lynbrook Challenge Success is a high school branch of Stanford’s Challenge Success team and operates as a student team working alongside PTSA to help reduce the high levels of stress on campus through creative activities and speakers. Our purpose is to bring awareness to students that a 4.0 GPA, 2400 SAT score, and 15 APs doesn’t measure one’s ability and happiness. “Success is measured through the course of a lifetime, not at the end of a semester.” Scroll down to read about our past events!

Challenge Success Fishbowl

A panel consisting of students in different grades and active in different extracurriculars was held in a form of Q + A fishbowl session. They were asked about their thoughts on school, homework, extracurriculars, expectations, pressures, and more. Parents were invited to gain an unshrouded understanding into the lives of their children. You can read the responses here: Challenge Success Fishbowl Trancript

Release Week

The week before AP testing, Challenge Success planned a week full of fun activities to help relieve the building stress in students and give them something to look forward to during the week. Our schedule for release week was:
Monday - Puppies in the Quad
Tuesday - Zumba
Wednesday - Bouncy Houses
Thursday - Free Root Beer Floats
Friday - Otter Pops
Check out our video here:

Monday - Puppies in the Quad

Challenge Success brought over 20 puppies onto campus during lunch and students walked around petting them. Students loved petting their furry friends!

Tuesday - Zumba

Challenge Success invited Jennette Holzworth, a professional trainer and nutrionist, to lead a Zumba workshop during lunch in the cove! Students came out to dance off their stress and try some silly moves for a change.

Wednesday - Bouncy Houses

Challenge Success brought in two bouncy activities: a bouncehouse and an obstacle course! Students could participate in both! For those that were not competitive, students jumped freely on their own in the bouncehouse. Other students raced their friends through the obstacle course! Many could be seen laughing their way across as they fell over themselves and got back up to finally crawl to the finish!

Thursday - Free Root Beer Floats

Free root beer floats were provided for students! PTSA parent volunteers helped out, and many students came to claim their savory root beer drink!

Friday - Otter Pops

What better way to end the week with some free otter pops? The perfect cure for the hot weather!
